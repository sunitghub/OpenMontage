# Blissful Strategy

Purpose: one operating guide for Blissful Chants strategy, production, visuals, long-form videos, and Shorts.

Use this first when deciding what to publish, how hard it will be to make, and which OpenMontage path to use.

Source basis:
- channel analytics window: `Jan 10, 2026` through `Apr 9, 2026`
- current-momentum window: `Mar 13, 2026` through `Apr 9, 2026`
- competitor references under `Design-Docs/Competitors/`
- current OpenMontage low-cost stack and Shorts workflow

## Executive Focus

Blissful Chants should stay centered on:
1. Maa Kali devotional utility
2. Maa Kali protection, kavach, stotra, mantra, vandana, and shatnaam
3. short Hindu spiritual tales with a clear devotional payoff
4. limited secondary lanes: Hanuman and Shiva first, other deities only as measured tests

Do not drift into generic horror. If a story uses ghosts, death, or spirits, it must resolve into Hindu spiritual meaning: karma, devotion, protection, rebirth, Maa Kali, Shiva, or afterlife teaching.

## Videos

Long-form and mid-form videos are the watch-time engine. They should feel devotional, repeatable, and useful.

### Video Format Table

| Format | Best Use | Target Runtime | Script Effort | Render Effort | Production Notes |
|---|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Kavach / stotra / mantra / shatnaam | Evergreen watch time | `6-14 min` | Low | Low | Lyrics/subtitles, sacred stills, slow local motion |
| Practical utility | Search and views | `2-5 min` | Medium | Low | Clear promise, numbered structure, simple visuals |
| Short utility video | Fast practical guidance | `20s-90s` | Low | Low | One ritual/offering/remedy per video |
| Devotee miracle story | Emotional discovery and depth | `3m30s-4m30s` | Medium | Medium | Still anchors plus `1-3` hero motion moments |
| Aatma / afterlife story | Story lane with spiritual payoff | `3m30s-6m` | Medium | Medium | Local VFX for soul, smoke, pyre, glow, lightning |
| Rural folk spiritual tale | Low-cost story volume | `3-6 min` | Medium | Low | Many illustrated cards, narration carries meaning |
| Secondary deity chant | Controlled expansion | `6-12 min` | Low | Low | Hanuman and Shiva first |

### 4-Minute Story Template

Use this for deity-helped-devotee, miracle, mythic lesson, and aatma stories.

| Part | Timing | Job |
|---|---:|---|
| Hook | `0-20s` | Immediate curiosity without disrespect |
| Setup | `20-60s` | Establish grief, fear, disorder, or question |
| Sacred Turn | `60-150s` | Divine sign, inner shift, karmic reveal, or protection moment |
| Human Relevance | `150-220s` | Tie the story to fear, surrender, ego, devotion, karma, or protection |
| Payoff | `220s+` | Land spiritual meaning, not just plot closure |

Story rules:
- keep the tone devotional, not horror-coded
- distinguish scripture, retelling, and devotional interpretation when needed
- avoid claims that sound like factual scripture unless corroborated
- one emotional spine per story
- one clear spiritual takeaway

### Story Scope Table

Use this to estimate how many scenes a story package needs before generating assets. The competitor story references average about one visual beat every `4-5s`: `Charava-Bhootni` used about `293` beats over `21:59`, and `Aatma-ChitaKeBaad` used about `310` beats over `21:42`. OpenMontage should not treat every beat as a paid video clip; most should be still anchors with local motion.

| Type | Target Runtime | Scene Count | Script Shape | Asset Strategy |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| Shorts | `35-75s` | `6-14` | Hook, setup, sacred/supernatural turn, takeaway | `6-14` stills/cards, `0-3` I2V hero clips depending on motion need |
| Video Small (`4 min`) | `3m30s-4m30s` | `40-60` | Hook, setup, rising tension, divine/karmic reveal, human relevance, payoff | `20-35` reusable still anchors plus local pans/zooms/VFX; `1-3` I2V hero clips |
| Video Long (`10+ min`) | `10-22 min` | `130-310` | Serialized folk/devotional story with repeated locations, character returns, turning points, and clear spiritual close | Batch still generation with a character/location bible; reusable local VFX; `3-8` I2V hero clips only for major reveals |

Planning rule:
- budget by hero moments, not by total scene count
- for a `4 min` story, script around `45-50` visual beats unless the narration is unusually slow
- for `10+ min`, build a reusable visual bible first, then repeat locations and character poses intentionally

## Shorts

Shorts are for discovery, testing topics, and turning long-form ideas into quick hooks.

### Shorts Format Table

| Short Type | Best Use | Target Runtime | Scene Count | I2V Clips | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Chant snippet | Light discovery, repeatable devotion | `14-30s` | `3-6` | `0-1` | Use one mantra line, strong caption, gentle motion |
| Practical utility Short | Ritual/offering/remedy teaser | `20-60s` | `4-8` | `0-1` | One clear answer, keyword title |
| Maa Kali story hook | Discovery and subscribers | `15-45s` | `4-8` | `1-2` | Lead with mystery or emotional need |
| Devotional symbol Short | Yantra, bija, symbol meaning | `35-75s` | `5-10` | `1-2` | One sacred focal point per scene |
| Folk-story card Short | Fast narrated moral tale | `35-75s` | `8-14` | `0-2` | Story cards, strong recurring costume/location anchors |
| Aatma / afterlife Short | Motion-sensitive supernatural teaching | `45-75s` | `8-12` | `2-3` | Use local VFX plus hero I2V |

### Shorts Workflow

Use the Shorts folder layout:

```text
Design-Docs/ToPublish/Shorts/<folder>/
  <folder>.md
  composition.html
  scene-1-bg.png
  scene-1-hero.mp4
  Renders/
    narration.mp3
    <folder>-captioned-v1.mp4
```

Minimum markdown structure:
- `Title`
- `Description`
- `Thumbnail`
- `Script`
- `Scenes`
- `Results` or `Artifact Status`

Phase flow:
1. Write or inspect `## Script`.
2. Generate `## Scenes` with `shorts-director`.
3. Write artifact prompts.
4. Generate only approved clips/assets.
5. Add voiceover in `Renders/`.
6. Build `composition.html`.
7. Render and QC.

### Shorts Cadence Rules

| Pattern | Cadence | Visual Rule |
|---|---|---|
| Devotional symbol montage | `4-6s` body holds | Dark sacred focal imagery; motion grows from symbol |
| Folk-story card montage | `3-5s` visual beats | Each frame is a story marker; narration carries plot |
| Aatma / afterlife motion-card | `4-6s` beats | Still anchors plus spirit/fire/smoke/glow layers |
| Hook scene | `4-5s` | Strong mystery, promise, or emotional question |
| Close | `4-8s` | Spiritual takeaway or devotional payoff |

## Production Stack

Use the low-cost stack by default.

| Layer | Default | Cost Posture |
|---|---|---|
| Stills | MidJourney, OpenAI/Grok/Recraft/Flux, or reused assets | cheap enough for most beats |
| Motion clips | Seedance 2.0 via Replicate for selected 5s I2V hero shots | about `$0.90/5s` from local billing |
| Local motion | Remotion or HyperFrames camera moves, parallax, particles, glow, smoke, text | `$0` API cost |
| Voice | HyperFrames TTS for draft; ElevenLabs/OpenAI/Google for branded output | low compared with video gen |
| Music | Existing library, Pixabay, ElevenLabs/Suno when needed | low compared with video gen |

Budget rule:
- `0` I2V clips: cheapest still-card animatic
- `1` I2V clip: hook or reveal, about `$0.90`
- `2` I2V clips: hook + peak, about `$1.80`
- `3` I2V clips: hook + mid-turn + peak, about `$2.70`
- everything else should be stills animated locally

Reference-specific mapping:
- Maa Kali chant/stotra/remedy: `0-1` I2V clips
- devotional symbol Short: `1-2` I2V clips
- Charava-Bhootni-style rural tale: `0-2` I2V clips
- Aatma-ChitaKeBaad-style afterlife tale: `2-3` I2V clips plus local VFX
- premium hero-only upgrade: compare Kling/Veo/Omni only for specific shots

Provider pricing changes quickly. Before paid generation, trust OpenMontage's live estimate and actual billing over old docs.

## Visual System

Blissful Chants should feel:
- reverent
- mystical
- luminous
- emotionally intense
- devotional first, cinematic second

Avoid:
- horror poster energy
- gore
- chaotic demon aesthetics
- random costume drift
- inconsistent deity faces
- typography that changes style scene to scene

### Visual Lock

Lock these before full generation:

| Element | Rule |
|---|---|
| Deity depiction | Reuse one face, skin tone, hair silhouette, ornaments, expression, aura logic |
| Style family | Devotional illustration, temple-mural digital painting, or sacred storybook 2D |
| Palette | Midnight indigo, divine crimson, ember orange, lotus gold, moon cream |
| Lighting | Low-key environment, divine rim light, sacred glow, incense haze |
| Framing | Deity close-up, medium portrait, full sacred silhouette, symbolic wide, devotee reaction, text impact card |
| Subtitles | Bottom-center safe area, short lines, one emphasis phrase at a time |

Maa Kali direction:
- deep blue-black skin
- calm but powerful divine expression
- flowing dark hair
- traditional ornaments rendered reverently
- crimson and lotus-gold aura
- powerful and compassionate even when fierce

Negative constraints to reuse:
- no gore
- no horror poster energy
- no monstrous distortion
- no costume drift
- no random extra limbs
- no chaotic background clutter

## Topic Rules

| If The Idea Is... | Then... |
|---|---|
| Offering, ritual, or devotional action | Make it utility-first and keyword-clear |
| Protection-focused or chant-led | Make it long enough for devotional immersion |
| Story, miracle, or emotional hook | Test it as a Short or 4-minute story first |
| Aatma, death, ghost, or afterlife | Resolve it into karma, rebirth, devotion, or spiritual teaching |
| Shiva | Treat as the best-supported secondary lane |
| Hanuman | Strong secondary lane for chalisa, protection, and remedies |
| Non-Kali/non-Shiva/non-Hanuman deity | Treat as a measured experiment |
| Generic ghost/horror | Deprioritize unless rewritten into devotional meaning |

## Title Rules

Use:
- deity keyword early
- concrete promise
- known search terms: `Kavach`, `Stotra`, `Mantra`, `Chalisa`, `Shatnaam`, `Divine Names`, `Offering`, `Protection`, `With Lyrics`

Avoid:
- vague poetic titles
- hiding the deity keyword
- broad spiritual wording when the topic is Kali-specific
- clickbait that misstates scripture or devotional meaning

## Thumbnail Rules

Every publishable package needs a ready thumbnail.

Thumbnail should have:
- deity or symbol as first-viewport signal
- readable title text
- strong devotional mood
- one dominant focal point
- no horror-coded styling unless the story explicitly needs fierce protective energy

## Package Rules

Each publishable project should have a reusable package with:
- title
- description
- script
- source/corroboration notes
- visual bible
- selected anchors
- scene plan
- artifact status
- render output
- thumbnail

Default long-form package location:
- `OpenMontage/projects/<project>/renders/<Render-Name>/`

Default Shorts package location:
- `OpenMontage/Design-Docs/ToPublish/Shorts/<folder>/`
