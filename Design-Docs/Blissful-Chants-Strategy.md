## Current Runtime Guidance

These are working targets from the exports, not permanent rules.

| Format | Current best use | Recommended target |
|---|---|---|
| Micro short | Quick story hook, chant line, devotional fact | `14s-30s` |
| Short utility video | Offering, ritual, practical tip | `20s-90s` |
| Mid-length utility video | Numbered list or guided devotional explainer | `2-5 min` |
| Long devotional video | Kavach, shatnaam, mantra, protection recitation | `6-14 min` |

## Operating Rules

| If... | Then... |
|---|---|
| The idea teaches an offering, ritual, or simple devotional action | Make it utility-first and keyword-clear |
| The idea is protection-focused or chant-led | Make it long enough to deliver devotional immersion |
| The idea is a story, miracle, or emotional hook | Test it short first |
| The idea is about Shiva | Treat it as the best-supported secondary lane |
| The idea is about a non-Kali, non-Shiva deity | Treat it as a measured experiment, not the channel default |

## Next-Step Recommendation

For the next publishing batch, prioritize:
1. one strong Maa Kali utility video with a concrete promise
2. one long-form Kali protection or chant video
3. one short `Real Stories`-style Kali hook
4. one Shiva devotional test only if bandwidth remains

## What To Deprioritize

| Content type | Why |
|---|---|
| Broad deity expansion as a main lane | Both the 90-day and 28-day windows are still overwhelmingly Kali-led |
| Uploads that depend on launch-day novelty alone | `Maa Gauri Namostute` spiked early and then lost momentum instead of compounding |
| Vague devotional titles without a clear promise | The strongest videos make a concrete promise: `5 Ways`, `Offering`, `Kavach`, `Gayatri`, `Stories` |
| Channel strategy built around assumptions not shown in analytics | The exports do not justify old claims about monetization state, tool budget, or rigid monthly cadence |

## Content Pillars To Keep

| Pillar | Primary goal | Best format now |
|---|---|---|
| Practical devotional utility | Views, search, repeat usefulness | `20s-5 min` |
| Short Kali stories and hooks | Discovery, subscribers | `15s-45s` |
| Protection, kavach, mantra, and name recitations | Watch time, evergreen value | `4-14 min` |
| Short chant snippets | Light discovery and devotional consistency | `15s-30s` |
| Limited Shiva devotional lane | Secondary watch-time expansion without losing channel focus | `6-12 min` |

## Title Patterns That Match The Data

| Pattern | Why it fits |
|---|---|
| `5 Ways To...` | Your strongest view leader uses a clear practical promise |
| `... Offering` / `... Offerings` | Offering videos show strong CTR and obvious devotional intent |
| `Real Stories of ...` | This format helped generate subscribers |
| `... Gayatri Mantra` | Established chant format with steady demand |
| `... Kavach` | Strong watch-time and clear protection promise |
| `... Divine Names` / `... Shatnaam` | Good fit for long-form devotional retention |
| `... Stotra \| With Lyrics` | Supported by the strong Shiva watch-time outlier |

Avoid:
- poetic but vague titles with no concrete devotional payoff
- titles that hide the deity keyword
- broad spiritual wording when the video is really about Kali-specific practice
