## Scene-1: 

    Is video main hum ek Sadhak ke anubhav sunege jisne Baglamukhi Sadhana apne Guru ke kehne par apne hi ghar main karen. Unke Guru ne yeh bhi kaha thaa ki Sadhana kis bhi chaudas ke din se shuru karni hai.
 
 ### English
 
     In this video, we will hear the experiences of a spiritual seeker who performed the Baglamukhi Sadhana within his own home, acting upon the instructions of his Guru. His Guru had also advised him to commence the Sadhana on any *Chauda*s (14th day of the lunar fortnight).

##Scene-2:
    Sadhak ne tay kar liya ki agle mahine ki Amavasy ke ek din se pehle woh apni Sadhana shuru karega. Usne apne ghar ke ek kamre ko is karya ke liye chun liya tha.
