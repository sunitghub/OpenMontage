## Script

### Hook

Most people see Maa Kali and stop at the fierce form.

But here are 5 powerful facts many devotees still do not know.

### Body

Fact one: Maa Kali is not a symbol of evil. She represents the power that destroys ego, fear, and illusion.

Fact two: her dark form is deeply symbolic. It points to the limitless cosmic reality beyond name and form.

Fact three: her tongue is not just shock imagery. Many devotees understand it as a moment of divine awareness, humility, or fierce protection depending on the tradition.

Fact four: standing on Shiva is not disrespect. It reveals a profound spiritual truth about Shakti and consciousness.

Fact five: even in her fiercest depictions, Maa Kali is worshipped by devotees as a loving mother who protects, liberates, and guides.

### Close

The fierce form is not the whole story.

For devotees, Maa Kali is power, truth, and compassion together.
